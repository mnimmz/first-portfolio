About

I am a designer & developer hybrid based in sunny Boca Raton, Florida. While studying Graphic Design at Palm Beach State College, I have been immersing myself in the world of front-end development during my spare time. Combining technology and design to showcase the essence of one’s ideas, makes what I do so gratifying. My undergraduate background in Communications and Marketing, as well as learning web development enables me to be a more skillful designer by approaching projects from a visual, textual and technical prospective. Aesthetically, I like my designs to be simple, yet vibrant. The important thing is for whatever project I’m working on, audiences understand the message, while finding its presentation visually pleasing. When I’m not designing, or coding, you’ll find me watching movies with my family, reading or catching up on a few of my favorite shows online.
